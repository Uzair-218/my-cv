About the Crisis in Gaza

Gaza, a densely populated area home to over 2 million people, has been under siege for years. Basic necessities like clean water, electricity, and medical care are scarce. The ongoing conflict has devastated countless lives.

In recent months, thousands of innocent civilians — many of them children — have been killed or displaced. This is a humanitarian tragedy, and silence is complicity.

We must raise our voices for peace, for justice, and for freedom.

“To be silent in the face of oppression is to side with the oppressor.”
– Desmond Tutu
